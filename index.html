Discover the Heritage of Malaysia's Oldest State

Kedah is widely recognised as one of Malaysia's oldest states, with a history spanning more than 2,800 years. From ancient archaeological discoveries to royal palaces and historic forts, the state preserves a remarkable legacy that reflects the development of early civilisation in Southeast Asia.

This website introduces ten historical places that represent Kedah's rich cultural heritage, allowing visitors to explore the stories, architecture, and traditions that continue to shape its identity today.
