About the Place

Gallery Archeotourism Kuala Muda introduces visitors to the archaeological and historical heritage of Kuala Muda through interactive exhibits and educational displays.



Historical Background

The gallery was established to promote public awareness of archaeological discoveries in Sungai Batu and the Bujang Valley, highlighting ancient trade routes and early civilisation in Kedah.

Historical Significance

  • Promotes archaeological tourism.
  • Educates visitors about Kedah's ancient civilisation.
  • Supports heritage conservation.
  • Serves as an educational centre for students and researchers.

Visitor Information

Visitors can explore interactive exhibitions featuring archaeological discoveries from Sungai Batu and the Bujang Valley. The gallery includes multimedia presentations, educational displays, and artefacts that explain the development of one of Southeast Asia's earliest civilisations, making it suitable for families, students, and researchers.
